HISTORY

 

The Jollibee logo and header image banner represent the rich history and milestones of our Jollibee Group, established in 1975 by Dr. Tony Tan Caktiong. Initially starting as a small family business selling ice cream in Manila, the vision expanded three years later to offer hot meals, leading to the transformation of ice cream parlors into the iconic Jollibee brand we recognize today.

Driven by Tony's vision and fueled by hard work, passion, and focus, the once modest enterprise has evolved into one of the largest companies in the Philippines, Asia, and globally. From a single brand, it has grown into a restaurant group encompassing 18 beloved brands with over 6,600 stores in 34 countries. Our commitment to spreading the joy of eating guides us as we strive to rank among the top five restaurant companies worldwide.
